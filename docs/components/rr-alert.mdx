## Accessibility

- **WAI-ARIA pattern:** [www.w3.org/WAI/ARIA/apg/patterns/alert/](https://www.w3.org/WAI/ARIA/apg/patterns/alert/)
- **Focusable:** no
- **Keyboard:** Tab moves focus to the dismiss button when dismissible; Enter or Space activates the dismiss button
- **WCAG:** 1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum), 1.4.11 Non-text Contrast, 4.1.3 Status Messages

**Rules**

- Do not rely on color alone to convey the alert variant — pair with a title or icon
- The dismiss button carries an aria-label of "Dismiss"
